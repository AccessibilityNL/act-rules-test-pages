Certification Prep

ACP exams are intended to be challenging.  Increase your chances of success with careful preparation!

  • Download Exam Topics and evaluate your readiness. 

  • Consult the Atlassian product documentation to learn about the nuances of each topic that you may be unfamiliar with.
