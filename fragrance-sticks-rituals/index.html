REED DIFFUSERS

SCENT DIFFUSER STICKS BY RITUALS

Reed diffusers are a subtle and stylish way to fragrance your home. Longer lasting than a candle and with no burning flame, they're also a practical choice. But you know that RITUALS doesn't do just 'practical'. Our luxury scent diffuser sticks present the finest concentrated essential oils and natural rattan reeds in sleek glass bottles or ceramic vases to add style to your home décor as they fragrance the air and uplift your mood.

LONG-LASTING, NATURAL SCENT DIFFUSERS

Tell us the effect you desire and we'll find you the perfect reed diffuser: the Ritual of Happy Buddha Fragrance Sticks with sweet orange and cedar to refresh and refine, the Ritual of Dao with the soft aroma of white lotus and yi-yi ren to calm body and soul, the Ritual of Hammam with eucalyptus and rosemary to restore and revitalise…all our collections have fragrance sticks. Each presenting 230ml of fragrance in a handsome glass phial with wooden cuff, they will last for around 3 months. To enhance the effect, try layering our fragrance sticks with scented candles from the same collection.

FRAGRANCE STICK MINI'S AND SUPERS

It's also nice to know that RITUALS top-selling reed diffuser fragrances are available as convenient, eco-friendly refills. Choose our Mini Fragrance Sticks to beautifully perfume smaller spaces such as bathrooms and cloakrooms. Or keep one on your desk to fill your personal workspace with wonderful uplifting aroma. For extra luxury or as a stunning gift, our Private Collection Fragrance Sticks come in stylish ceramic vases and will infuse your space with authentic and sophisticated scents for up to 5 months.

THE PERFECT GIFT – WRAPPED & DELIVERED

Beautifully packaged in card cylinders or boxed, RITUALS fragrance sticks make wonderful gifts. Shop online here at our official store for fragrance sticks as birthday presents, 'thank you' gifts and other spoiling surprises. If you like, we can even wrap your gift and deliver it direct with your personal message.
